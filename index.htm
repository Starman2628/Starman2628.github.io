Game Concept

Game Title: Ball Battle​ Platformer: 3D game​ High-Level Concept:​ A 3D multiplayer maze game where two players compete to navigate three balls to their respective destinations. Players unlock and guide one ball at a time, facing various obstacles and strategic challenges. The first player to successfully guide all three balls to their destinations wins the game.​ ​ Genre:​ Primary Genre: Puzzle-Action​ Sub-Genres: Multiplayer, Strategy​ ​ Gameplay:​ Objective: Navigate three balls through a maze to their respective destinations.​ Controls: Use 360-degree Gyro/Motion Control to steer the balls.​ Obstacles: Avoid blades, water, fire blazes, and wind.​ Power-Ups: Collect power stones to temporarily block the opponent’s progress.​ Progression: Advance by successfully guiding balls through increasingly challenging mazes. Unlock new maze levels.​ Core Loop:​ Primary Actions: Tilt and turn the mobile device to navigate the ball through the maze, avoid or overcome obstacles, collect power stones, and outmaneuver your opponent.​ Rewards: 100 XP for each ball successfully guided to its destination, unlocking new textures and particle effects.​ ​ ​ Target Audience:​ 8 and above​ Market research: Fans of strategy and puzzle games who appreciate a mix of skill-based gameplay and strategic decision-making.​ ​ Unique Selling Point (USP)​ Dynamic Obstacle Interaction: Players must skillfully navigate a series of ever-changing obstacles, each with unique effects, adding a layer of strategy and timing.​ Power Stone Mechanic: Collecting power stones to temporarily block the opponent's progress introduces a strategic element to the race.​ ​ Player Experience and Game POV​ Player's Role: Player control three balls, navigating them through a challenging maze filled with hazards.​ Game Perspective: Third-person isometric view for better visibility of the maze.​ Key Emotions: Tension, excitement, and satisfaction as players outmaneuver obstacles and opponents.​
